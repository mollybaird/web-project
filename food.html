How To Make A Cake

Instructions

  1. Make the Batter

    • Get out each ingredient needed for the recipe
    • Follow the recipe and combine the ingredients

  2. Put the Batter in a Pan

    • Make sure to line the pan or spray it with oil

  3. Put the Pan in the Oven

    • Preheat oven to desired temperature
    • Place cake in the oven
    • Set timer for allotted baking time
    • Once timer is finished take the cake out
    • After cake has cooled decorate it as you wish
